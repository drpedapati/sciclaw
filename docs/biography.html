Research and software philosophy

Dr. Pedapati’s work sits at the intersection of clinical psychiatry, translational neuroscience, and practical engineering. The emphasis is simple: scientific rigor should be reproducible by default, and modern AI tools should strengthen investigator judgment, not replace it.

sciClaw reflects that philosophy in software form, local-first workflows, transparent plain-text configuration, evidence trails, and tools that fit established academic practice instead of forcing researchers into opaque platforms.
