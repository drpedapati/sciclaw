Your Paired Scientist

Imagine getting revisions from a trusted mentor: tracked changes in your draft, a fresh stack of key papers, all while keeping your own cadence.

Science isn’t a sprint—it’s a deliberate rhythm of questioning, refining, and verifying. Unlike business tools chasing speed at any cost, sciClaw honors that pace. And unlike a black-box platform, every part of how it thinks and works lives in plain text files you can read, edit, and version.

A Partnership, Not a Platform

Most AI agents treat scientists like users logging into someone else’s system—your data hosted remotely, workflows rushed, control surrendered. We built sciClaw differently: a personal, local paired scientist that runs on your machine.

It handles what machines excel at—searching PubMed, drafting methods, logging every step—while leaving the human magic to you: deep reasoning, ethical judgment, and the intuitive sense of order that only you bring.

No hosted service. No proprietary lock-in. Just plain files in ~/sciclaw you version with git, audit anytime, and share with collaborators. You’re always in the loop, teaching it your protocols via simple markdown—like onboarding a postdoc.

You Shape It with Plain Text

sciClaw’s personality, knowledge, and boundaries aren’t hidden in a database. They’re markdown files in your workspace that you edit like any other document:

SOUL.md is where you define what the agent values—epistemic humility, reproducibility over convenience, transparent uncertainty. It’s the conscience. IDENTITY.md gives it a sense of who it is: your lab’s research partner, not a generic chatbot.

USER.md is about you—your communication preferences, your target journal, how much detail you want in evidence summaries. AGENTS.md defines the research loop itself: frame the question, propose a plan, execute with tools, update findings. The scientific method, encoded.

MEMORY.md is the lab notebook that persists across sessions—active hypotheses, rejected ideas, open questions, confidence levels. When you pick up work a month later, the context is still there. HOOKS.md ensures every tool call, every decision point, every error is captured in an audit trail you can revisit.

And TOOLS.md governs how the agent uses its capabilities: tools serve the methodology, operations should be reversible, every output links back to the evidence that produced it.

Seven files. All readable. All yours to change. That’s the entire operating system for your paired scientist.

The Scientist’s Cadence

Life science moves at biology’s tempo: iterative, cautious, reproducible. Tools from faster-moving fields often mismatch—opaque, cloud-dependent, velocity over verifiability.

sciClaw gets it. It delivers mentor-like support—tracked Word edits, provenance logs, citation chains—without disrupting your flow. Six months later, retrace exactly how a result emerged. That’s trust built for discovery, not disruption.

Humans + Machines: Complementary Strengths

Machines crunch data; humans weave meaning. Human-centered AI research reminds us that we thrive when AI augments our values—control, context, well-being.

sciClaw embodies this: you set the guardrails in SOUL.md, define your preferences in USER.md, track your reasoning in MEMORY.md, and the agent executes reliably within those boundaries. It’s your extension, prioritizing what you do best.

Open source. Free. Yours to shape. Science waits for no one, but it demands a partner like this.
